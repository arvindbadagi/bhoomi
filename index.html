Welcome to the Revenue Department of Karnataka

Ensuring Transparent and Efficient Administration of Land Records and Revenue Services.

Streamlining Land Services for Citizens

Providing seamless access to land-related information and services.

Modernizing Revenue Administration

Embracing technology to enhance efficiency and transparency.

    Our Key Services

    We provide a range of essential services to the citizens of Karnataka.

    01

    Land Records Management

    Maintain and update land records, including RTC, MR, and Mutation.

    02

    Land Conversion Services

    Facilitate the process of converting land from agricultural to non-agricultural use.

    03

    Bagair Hukum Processing

    Process applications related to unauthorized cultivation of government lands.

    04

    Pahani Online | i-RTC

    Get original Record of Rights online, pay Rs. 15 and access RTC from anywhere.

    05

    View RTC And MR

    View any survey number RTC, including present and old RTC records.

    06

    Verify Linked RTCs to your Aadhar

    Check and link lands associated with your Aadhaar, complete Aadhaar linking with live photo.

    07

    Parihara

    Efficient compensation for farmers affected by crop loss through streamlined technology.

    08

    Kaveri

    Enhanced property registration with intuitive interface, streamlined processes, and improved security.

    09

    Bhoomi

    Digital land records system converting manual RTCs to efficient digital formats, enhancing transparency.
